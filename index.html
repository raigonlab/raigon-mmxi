Railson Viana
Artist · Tattooist · Designer · Zürich

He develops work at the intersection of art, form, and digital systems. His practice explores structure, rhythm, and visual language through abstraction and continuous flow, treating the body as a canvas for conceptual composition.

Based in Zürich, his work spans digital drawing, tattooing, and visual design — each discipline feeding into a singular pursuit of form. Collections are built through long cycles of observation, reduction, and mark-making that seek precision without losing breath.
